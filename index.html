Your Dreams

No Clothes

I was at school, not one I recognized and I didn't have any clothes on. Thankfully I was wearing underwear. I spent the day hiding about bathrooms and closets to avoid people seeing me bare it all.

Hanging out with Justin Timberlake

In this dream I was related to Justin Timberlake. He was my uncle and him and his wife were expecting their first born child soon. My family and I attended one of his concerts and met with him afterwards.

Falling off a Cliff

I was running away from a creature in the woods. It did not have any distinct features. It was bipedal and a dark black mass, though it was also quite lanky. The black was a dark aura that flittered about this person. I ran so far that I made in through and before I knew it I had ran off a cliff. I was screaming and flailing and eventually everything turned black, but I continued to fall.

No Voice

I was swimming at the beach. It was a bright and sunny day. My family was back on shore. I was fairly far out. Treading water because my feet were far from the bottom. Suddenly I spotted a narrow fin swerving about. I began to panic but in the moment I felt it was best to keep still. I tried to yell but nothing came out. The shark was circling and I was forcing my voice as hard as I could but no one could hear me.

Hide and Seek Nightmare

I was in a creeky dark and old house. There was a person there. I'm not sure man or woman, but they were looking for me. Though it was not meant to be a game of hide and seek it turned into me finding different spots in the house to hide from this person. Everything got dark and they finally found me. They had on heavy combat boots. I was on the ground helpless and they stabbed me.

Party for the President

I was in a very grand ballroom, covered in soft red velvet. There were many people there. The men in sharp black tuxedo suits and the women in 1800s era gowns with big hair. I was there also dressed like these women. This was a party for the president and everyone was waiting for him to arrive. I was on my way out of the party and ran into a man and it was the president. I shook his hand and told him about my political ideas.
